What is an economic feature?

An economic feature is a characteristic or aspect of an economy that helps to distinguish it from other economies. Economic features can include a wide range of characteristics, such as the type of goods and services that are produced, the level of development of the economy, the level of technological advancement, the level of foreign trade, the level of income and wealth distribution, the type of economic system in place, and the level of government intervention in the economy. Economic features can have significant impacts on the functioning and performance of an economy, and they are often used to compare and contrast different economies.
