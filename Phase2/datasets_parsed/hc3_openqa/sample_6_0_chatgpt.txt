who is flo from progressive

Flo is a fictional character and the spokesperson for Progressive Insurance, an American insurance company. Flo is known for her distinctive uniform and perky personality, and has appeared in a series of television commercials for Progressive since 2008. The character was created by the advertising agency Arnold Worldwide, and is portrayed by actress and comedian Stephanie Courtney. Flo is meant to embody the helpful and friendly nature of Progressive, and is often seen assisting customers with their insurance needs in the company's advertisements.
