The United States is a federal presidential constitutional republic comprising 50 states and a capital district. The country is located in North America and is bordered by Canada to the north and Mexico to the south. The United States is the world's third- or fourth-largest country by total area, depending on how it is measured. It is one of the world's largest and most economically and technologically advanced countries, and has a diverse population with immigrants from many different countries and cultures. The United States has a long history and has played a significant role in global affairs for more than two centuries. The country has a federal system of government with a president as the head of state and a two-party system of politics. The economy of the United States is the world's largest and most technologically advanced, and it is a leader in many industries, including technology, finance, and healthcare.
