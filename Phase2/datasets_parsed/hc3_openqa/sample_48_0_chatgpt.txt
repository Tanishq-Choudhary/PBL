what kind of cut is tri tip

Tri-tip is a cut of beef from the bottom sirloin section of the cow. It is a triangular-shaped muscle that is lean and flavorful, and it is typically grilled or roasted. It is a popular cut of beef in the United States, especially on the West Coast, and it is often served thinly sliced and served in sandwiches or on salads. It is also sometimes referred to as triangle steak, sirloin tip, or bottom sirloin roast.
