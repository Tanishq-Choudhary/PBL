He engaged in negotiations with Soviet leader Mikhail Gorbachev and signed the Intermediate-Range Nuclear Forces (INF) Treaty, which called for the elimination of intermediate-range nuclear missiles. \n\nReagan is also remembered for his efforts to improve relations with the Soviet Union and for his role in helping to bring about the end of the Cold War.  He is known for a number of things, including his conservative political ideology, his efforts to reduce government regulations and taxes, and his foreign policy, particularly his stance towards the Soviet Union. \n\nReagan is often considered one of the most influential presidents in American history and is remembered as a strong leader who helped to restore confidence in the United States during a time of economic uncertainty and international tension.
