what are warehouse spreadsheets used for Warehouse spreadsheets are used to track and manage various aspects of a warehouse's operations.  Warehouse spreadsheets may include information such as the quantity and location of items in the warehouse, the status of orders and shipments, and other relevant data.  This can include inventory management, order fulfillment, shipping and receiving, and various other tasks.  They can be used to help warehouse managers and employees stay organized and efficient, and to produce informed decisions about the warehouse's operations.
