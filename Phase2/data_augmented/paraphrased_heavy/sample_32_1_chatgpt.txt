What is an economic feature?  An economic feature is a characteristic or aspect of an economy that affects how it functions and produces, distributes, and consumes goods and services.  Economic features can also include the types of industries and sectors that are present in the economy, the level of employment and unemployment, and the level of inflation.  Economic features can include various factors such as the type of economic system, the level of economic development, the level of technological development, the level of international trade, the level of government intervention, the level of income inequality, and the distribution of resources.  Understanding economic features is vital for policymakers, businesses, and individuals in order to make informed decisions about economic policy, investment, and consumption.
