What did Harry Potter have that made it such a massively successful series ? In other words , what were the factors which lead to the unrivaled success of Harry Potter ? Please explain like I'm five.

Harry Potter is a series of books and movies about a young boy named Harry who discovers that he is a wizard and goes to a school for magic. The series became very popular because it had an exciting and engaging story with relatable characters, and it was able to appeal to readers and viewers of all ages. 
One of the main reasons for the series' success was that it was able to capture the imagination of readers and keep them engaged with its complex and well-developed plot. The books and movies also featured memorable characters, such as Harry, Ron, and Hermione, who readers could root for and relate to. 
Additionally, the Harry Potter series was able to build a strong and dedicated fan base through its immersive and detailed world-building. The series featured a rich and detailed magical universe with its own rules, customs, and history, which made it easy for readers to get lost in the story and become invested in the characters and their adventures. 
Overall, the combination of an exciting and engaging story, relatable characters, and immersive world-building helped make Harry Potter a massively successful series that continues to be loved by readers and viewers around the world.
