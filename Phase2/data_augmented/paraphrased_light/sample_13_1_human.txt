What is a " Tor exit node " or " Tor node " What is a " Tor exit node " or " Tor node " ? Please explain like I'm five. Tor is an encryption system that bounces your data between dozens of computers around the world , each computer unravelling one layer of encryption that tells it who to send it to next , to produce it tough to trace . Each one of those computers is a node , and the one at the end of the chain - where your data hits the rest of the internet - is an exit node .
