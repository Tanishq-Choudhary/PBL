what food is in afghan Afghan cuisine is a blend of Middle Eastern and Central Asian flavors. Some common ingredients and dishes found in Afghan cuisine include:\n\nRice: Rice is a staple in Afghan cuisine and is often served with meals.\n\nNaan: Naan is a type of flatbread that is commonly eaten in Afghanistan. It is often served with meals as a side dish.\n\nKebabs: Kebabs are a popular dish in Afghanistan and are made with grilled meat, typically lamb or chicken, and served with rice or naan.\n\nBolani: Bolani is a type of flatbread filled with vegetables, such as potatoes, onions, or leeks, and served with yogurt or chutney.\n\nAshak: Ashak is a dish made with boiled dumplings filled with a mixture of leeks and ground beef, and served with a yogurt sauce.\n\nQabili palaw: Qabili palaw is a dish made with rice and lamb, and is often garnished with raisins and carrots.\n\nAush: Aush is a type of soup made with noodles, meat, and vegetables, and is often served with a side of yogurt.\n\nFruits and vegetables: Afghanistan is home to a variety of fresh fruits and vegetables, including melons, grapes, pomegranates, apples, apricots, and tomatoes. These are often used in Afghan dishes or eaten as snacks.
