what kind of cut is tri tip Tri-tip is a cut of beef from the bottom sirloin primal cut. It is a triangular shaped muscle that is lean and tender when cooked properly. It is often grilled or roasted and is popular in the cuisine of the western United States, particularly in California. The tri-tip is a versatile cut of meat that can be sliced thin for sandwiches or cut into thicker slices for grilling or roasting. It is typically seasoned with a rub or marinade before cooking and is best served medium-rare to medium.
