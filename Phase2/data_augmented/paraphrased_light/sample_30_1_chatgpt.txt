where do the mohawks live The Mohawk people are an indigenous group who originally inhabited the Mohawk Valley in upstate New York. They are part of the Haudenosaunee (Iroquois) Confederacy, which includes six tribes: the Mohawk, Oneida, Onondaga, Cayuga, Seneca, and Tuscarora. The Mohawk people also have a significant presence in Canada, with reserves located in Ontario and Quebec. There are also Mohawk communities in other parts of the United States, including Wisconsin, Oklahoma, and the Northeast.
